My Future Fashion Sense

Nell Przepiora

Era

My style in fashion is heavily inspired by fashion culture in the 90's to the 2000's, ranging from haute couture styles to grunge and y2k elements.

Elements

Landmarks of my taste in fashion range in varying textures, such as metals, leather, sequins, and lace. Some elements that I tend to gravitate towards would include but not be limited to:
  • Metal spikes
  • Crosses
  • Ragged and weathered textures
  • Gems and studs
  • Metallic sheens
  • Dark and saturated colors
  • Animal prints, such as cheetah and leopard
  • Layered and thin fabrics
